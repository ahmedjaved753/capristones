### 2026-05-06 — Home-page hero carousel (3-slide auto-advancing quarry imagery)

Replaced the static single-image hero on `HomePage.jsx` with a 3-slide auto-advancing carousel composed in a new `src/components/HeroCarousel.jsx`. Imagery sourced from `capristones.com`'s Elementor hero slideshow (3 quarry photographs — open-pit, drilling-rig extraction, mountain panorama — downloaded via Playwright MCP at the highest resolution available on their CDN, 2500×1680 / 2500×1667 / 1000×475). The carousel's design intent is editorial / gallery-grade, not "Bootstrap carousel": the editorial frame (eyebrow, H1 "Surfaces Built / *to Last*", sub-paragraph, two CTAs) is passed in as `children` and stays completely static while the slabs change underneath. Slides crossfade every 6s (1.2s expo-out, `cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)`); the active slide gets a slow Ken Burns scale 1 → 1.06 over the full 7.2s lifetime; reduced-motion users get a 0.2s cut and no scale. Differentiators vs a generic carousel: (a) **museum-placard index strip** at the bottom — three columns with italic warm-sienna numerals "01 / 02 / 03", display-serif title, body uppercase meta — replacing bullet dots; (b) **per-column hairline that doubles as the auto-advance progress bar**, terracotta `bg-accent` `h-px` filling left-to-right under the active column; (c) **asymmetric corner-anchored radial scrim** sized to the lower-left text area only — preserves the right side and top of every slide instead of darkening the whole image; (d) faint SVG-noise grain at 6% opacity for atmosphere. Pause/play toggle (`aria-pressed`) sits at the right end of the index strip; full keyboard model (←/→ to step, the toggle is tab-reachable); auto-advance pauses on hover, on `document.visibilitychange` (hidden tab), and during user-toggled pause; live region announces the active slide name on change (`aria-live="polite"`, `aria-atomic="true"`). Mobile collapses the placard to 3 segmented bars + the active caption. Captions follow the brand-story thread "The Quarry / The Block / The Range" with origin-neutral meta — no country other than Brazil is named, in line with the single-origin sourcing stance (see CLAUDE.md "Pricing & sourcing rules"). Old retired chrome: the bottom-left vertical "Scroll" cue was removed because the index strip is a richer scroll affordance and the two would have collided. Visual-test infrastructure: the global `mask: [page.locator('img')]` rule in `tests/visual.spec.js` was loosened to `mask: [page.locator('img:not(.hero-slide-img)')]` so the hero imagery — which IS the design — appears in the `home-hero` baseline snapshot. The 3 carousel imgs carry the marker class for the exemption. `tests/README.md` updated to document the carve-out. 1 visual baseline regenerated (`home-hero`); the other 18 unchanged.
